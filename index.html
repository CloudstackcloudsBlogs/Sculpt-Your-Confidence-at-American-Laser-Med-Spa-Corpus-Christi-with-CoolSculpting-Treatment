What to Expect with CoolSculpting

CoolSculpting is a specialized method of fat reduction that uses controlled cooling to target fat cells beneath the skin. Approved by the FDA, it works through a process called cryolipolysis. This means fat cells are frozen, damaged, and then naturally removed by the body over time.

It’s a non-invasive option, which appeals to many who don’t want surgery or long recovery periods. The treatment is ideal for those who are close to their desired weight but want to improve the shape of specific areas like the abdomen, thighs, love handles, or under the chin.

Here’s how a typical treatment session goes:

  • Consultation – You’ll meet with one of our specialists to discuss your goals, evaluate if you’re a candidate, and develop a personalized treatment plan.
  • Treatment – A gel pad and applicator are placed on the treatment area. The device delivers cooling that targets fat while keeping surrounding tissues safe.
  • Post-Treatment – After the session, you’re free to resume your day. There’s no required downtime, and most people experience only mild redness or tenderness.

Patients usually begin to notice changes within a few weeks, with more visible improvements developing over one to three months.

Common Treatment Areas and Goals

CoolSculpting is often selected to treat areas where stubborn fat tends to linger, even with regular exercise and balanced nutrition. These areas include:

  • Abdomen – For smoothing out the midsection.
  • Flanks (Love Handles) – To create a more contoured waistline.
  • Thighs – For improved symmetry in both inner and outer thighs.
  • Under the Chin – For defining the jawline and reducing a double chin.
  • Upper Arms – To reduce excess fat and help create a sleeker profile.
  • Back or Bra Area – To minimize bulges and create a smoother silhouette.

Most people treat more than one area across several sessions, based on their personal goals and the recommendations made during their consultation.
